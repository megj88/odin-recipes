The best sandwich in the world

Ingredients

2 tbsp ricotta
1 tbsp grated parmesan
50g mozzarella
2 thick slices of bread
25g gorgonzola (or a non-smelly cheese of choice
1 tbsp mayonnaise
1 tbsp butter

Method

  1. Mix the ricotta, parmesan and mozzarella and spread the mix over the slices of bread. Top with the third cheese, then close the sandwich.

  2. Spread the outside of the sandwich with mayo, then fry the sandwich in butter until cheese is melted and outside is golden brown.
